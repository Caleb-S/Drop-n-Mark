Recommended settings based on your browser.



Root Folder

Choose your root folder; folders within the root folder will be available in your bookmark menu to save new bookmarks & folders. If your browsers Chrome, root 1 is used by your browsers bookmark bar, in other browsers such as brave your bookmark bar folder may be Root 2.


Sort File & Folders

Move all folders to Root folder and move files not in a folder to other root folder - can not be undone.

Remove Duplicates

Will remove all duplicate bookmarks
